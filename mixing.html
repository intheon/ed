Mixing

Mixing facilities can be the burden of many records; be it that the budget runs out on renting a facility or that facility just can’t produce the results. As with all stages, mixing demands the proper approach, respect and physical space.

I have options to suit all budgets but install pride in the mixing and mastering suite I built in my own home for the ease of each record made. It’s important to be comfortable, settled and as un-tainted by deadlines or financial strains as possible (as possible being the operative word here) and when it comes to mixing having such an intimate, personal and familiar space aids the completion of the music and allows us to work in the now all-too-thematic-approach of getting things right for the song.

As we must always know, the song is above us as humans. It is an entity unto itself and we bring it into existence through our minds, instruments and technologies. In mixing we assess all the elements and place them where they belong to produce the whole.
