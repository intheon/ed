Mastering

Mastering brings a lot of joy into my life. It can be described as the ‘dark art’ of audio and is probably the most commonly misunderstood process. Mastering is the final stage before tracks are submitted away from the studio realms; it is the last alteration to the sound of a record before the music and audio is considered ‘complete’ and ensures the music is tonally balanced and competitively loud for appliance in the every day world.

The perspective an engineer can have on a record when being hired to objectively decide the best outcomes for a mix, already prepared, is incredibly satisfying for me… To hone in on the strengths and reduce the weaknesses and to bring out the best in someone else’s already beautiful work; there is no job satisfaction quite like this for me.
